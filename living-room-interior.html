Best Living Room Interior Design in Bangalore

At Builtlineinteriornivas, we specialize in creating modern and elegant living room interiors that reflect your personality and lifestyle. Our expert designers focus on space planning, furniture selection, lighting and decor to transform your living room into a comfortable and stylish space.

A well-designed living room is the heart of every home. We ensure a perfect balance of functionality and aesthetics by using high-quality materials, smart layouts and creative design concepts. Whether you prefer a modern, luxury or minimal style, we customize every detail to match your needs.

What We Include in Living Room Design

  • ✔ Space planning and furniture layout
  • ✔ TV unit and wall panel design
  • ✔ Sofa selection and arrangement
  • ✔ False ceiling and lighting design
  • ✔ Wall colors, textures and wallpapers
  • ✔ Curtains, decor and accessories

We aim to deliver living room interiors that are not only visually appealing but also practical for everyday living. With our professional approach and attention to detail, we ensure complete customer satisfaction.

TV Unit Design

A well-designed TV unit enhances the overall look of your living room while providing smart storage solutions. At Builtline, we create modern TV unit designs that combine functionality with style. From wall-mounted units to customized panel designs, we ensure your entertainment space looks clean, organized and visually appealing. Our designs include storage cabinets, display shelves and elegant finishes that perfectly match your interiors.

Center Table

The center table is the focal point of your living room, adding both style and functionality. We offer a wide range of modern and elegant center table designs in wood, glass and premium finishes. Our designs are carefully selected to complement your sofa and overall décor, creating a balanced and sophisticated look. Whether you prefer minimal or luxury styles, we provide solutions that enhance your space.

Sofa Design

A comfortable and stylish sofa is essential for every living room. We design and provide premium quality sofas that offer both comfort and elegance. From L-shaped and sectional sofas to compact seating solutions, our designs are tailored to suit your space and lifestyle. We focus on high-quality materials, modern designs and perfect color combinations to create a welcoming and luxurious seating area.

Chairs

Accent chairs play an important role in enhancing the overall aesthetics of your living room. Our collection includes modern, classic and contemporary chair designs that add character and comfort to your space. Whether used for additional seating or decorative purposes, our chairs are designed to blend seamlessly with your interiors while providing maximum comfort and style.

Living Room Partitions

Living room partitions are a smart way to divide spaces while maintaining an open and elegant look. We design modern partitions using wood, glass and creative materials to enhance both functionality and style. Our partition designs help separate areas like dining and living spaces without making the room feel closed. Each design is customized to match your home’s interiors and layout.
