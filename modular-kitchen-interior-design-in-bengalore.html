01

Straight Kitchen

Straight kitchens are simple and space-saving layouts where all elements are arranged along a single wall. This design is ideal for small homes, studio apartments and minimalistic interiors. We create straight kitchens that are both functional and stylish, ensuring efficient use of space with smart storage solutions and modern finishes.

02

L-Shaped Kitchen

L-shaped kitchens are one of the most popular and practical kitchen layouts, designed to efficiently utilize corner spaces. This layout creates a smooth work triangle between cooking, cleaning and storage areas, ensuring better functionality. Our L-shaped kitchen designs focus on maximizing space while maintaining a clean, modern and spacious appearance.

03

Parallel Kitchens

Parallel kitchens, also known as galley kitchens, are designed with two parallel countertops that create a highly efficient workflow. This layout is perfect for compact homes and apartments, offering maximum functionality in limited space. Our parallel kitchen designs ensure proper storage, ventilation and ease of movement while maintaining a modern look.

04

U-shaped Kitchens

U-shaped kitchens provide maximum storage and workspace by utilizing three walls, making them ideal for larger families and frequent cooking needs. This layout offers ample cabinets, countertops and efficient movement within the kitchen. We design U-shaped kitchens that combine convenience, durability and a stylish finish for a complete cooking experience.

05

Island Kitchens

Island kitchens are a modern and luxurious choice for contemporary homes, offering a central workspace that enhances both functionality and style. This design provides additional counter space, storage and even seating, making it perfect for open-plan living. At Builtline, we create customized island kitchens that improve workflow while adding a premium and elegant look to your home.
